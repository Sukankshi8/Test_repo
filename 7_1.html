We suffer from depression at some point in our life but unfortunately
it is not treated.

There are some similarities between sadness and depression but they are
very different to each other. Sad person knows what is wrong but depressed
person doesn't know what is it, which is making them depressed.

The only way to get out of depression is to see a therapist.
Follow your treatment plan. Find a routine and stick with it.
Declutter your mind. Stop worrying and try to communicate with your loved ones.
Those who never ask for help will never be able to get out of it.

So ask out, reach out to people whom you trust.
